Homestay Operations Reset

Role: Operations manager and systems designer

Context: Seasonal pressure created variability in service consistency, communication, and cost control.

Problem: Team response depended on individuals, not process, leading to fragile execution under stress.

Approach: Introduced SOPs, incident paths, inventory checkpoints, and weekly review loops.

Outcome: Stabilized service delivery and made operational performance easier to measure.

Lessons: Calm systems are built before crisis, not during it.
