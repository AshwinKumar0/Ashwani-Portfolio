Operational Dashboard Prototype

Role: Product thinker and front-end builder

Context: Homestay operations required daily clarity over occupancy, supplies, and issue escalation.

Problem: Decisions were reactive because the same information lived across calls, notebooks, and memory.

Approach: Designed a single-screen JavaScript prototype with grouped metrics, alert thresholds, and weekly trend views.

Outcome: Reduced decision lag and introduced repeatable weekly planning routines.

Lessons: A simple interface can outperform a complex one when it mirrors real operational decisions.
