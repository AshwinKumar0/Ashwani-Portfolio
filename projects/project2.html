Context: Long-format comic episodes needed consistent readability while preserving mood and pacing.

Problem: Inconsistent framing and transition rhythm diluted story impact across episodes.

Approach: Built a structured editing checklist around panel rhythm, focal hierarchy, and visual continuity.

Outcome: Improved narrative cohesion and reduced revision cycles for serialized releases.

Lessons: Story quality scales when creative work is supported by explicit systems.
